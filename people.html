Director

Xiaorong Zhang, Ph.D.

Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering · San Francisco State University

Professor Zhang joined SFSU in 2013. Her research spans human–machine interfaces, neural-controlled artificial limbs, embedded systems, and intelligent computing technologies. She received an NSF Faculty Early Career Development (CAREER) Award for work on next-generation neural–machine interfaces for EMG-controlled neurorehabilitation.

She earned her Ph.D. and M.S. in Computer Engineering from the University of Rhode Island and her B.S. in Computer Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology.
