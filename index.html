About NEES Lab

Where neuroengineering meets deployable computing.

Directed by Professor Xiaorong Zhang at San Francisco State University, the NEES Lab takes a computer engineering approach to human-centered health technology. Our work spans sensing, machine learning, embedded systems, and real-time interfaces.

We emphasize systems that can leave the laboratory: robust, low-cost, responsive, and designed with the people and communities they serve in mind.
